<h2>PL Assignment 1</h2>
<p>-Syntax: Aspects of a programming language that can be modeled 
by a context free grammar.</p>
<p>-Semantics: Rules of a programming language that help us interpret 
the meaning of a given string.</p>
<p>-Lexical Scanning: Converting sequences of single character tokens to a set of 
units that have their own meaning.</p>
<p>-Parsing and Parser: Explaining a sentence through its grammatical derivation that is
